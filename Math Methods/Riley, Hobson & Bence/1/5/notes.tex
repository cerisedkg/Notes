\textbf{Def:} The \textbf{binomial expansion} is a general method for expanding functions in the form of $(x+y)^n$

\subsection{Method:}
\begin{equation}
(x+y)^n = \sum_{k=0}^{n} {n\choose k}x^{n-k}y^k = \sum_{k=0}^{n} {n\choose k}x^{k}y^{n-k}
\end{equation}
