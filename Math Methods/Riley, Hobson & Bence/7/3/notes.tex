\textbf{Def:} A \textbf{scalar multiplication} is a vector multiplied by a scaling factor. Multiplication by scalar will not change the vector's angle, only its magnitude, but multiplication by a negative scalar will flip the vector's direction.

Vector multiplication is distributive, associative, and commutative.
