\begin{definition}[Inverse]
The \textbf{nverse} of a square matrix $A$ is a matrix $A ^{-1} $ such that $A A ^{-1}  = A ^{-1} A = I$.
\end{definition}

The elements of an inverse matrix are determined by: $$ (A ^{-1} )_{ij} = \frac{C_{ki}}{\vert A \vert}$$
