\begin{definition}[Transpose of a Matrix]
The \textbf{transpose} of a matrix $A$ is found by swapping the rows and colums in $A$  
\end{definition}

$(ABCDEFG...N)^T = N^T...G^TF^TE^TD^TC^TB^TA^T$
